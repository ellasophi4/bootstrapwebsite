About Us:

The Poison Mobile Speakeasy's mission is to transform your event into a vibrant one, by offering custom mobile cocktail bars & drinks packages for hire at home to your personal taste. We are a group of professionally trained mixologists sharing the same passion for craft cocktails and private parties. Whether you need to hire bartenders for the evening or want us to provide an all-inclusive drinks package, Poison Mobile Speakeasy would love to be part of it. With our party packages, we can create a drinks menu tailored to your personal taste. Alcoholic non-alcoholic cocktails are both included, our bespoke cocktail packages are made with homemade syrups, infused spirits, and fresh herbs. Holding an event in Bath or Bristol? Hire us!
